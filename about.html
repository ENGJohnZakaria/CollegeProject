Welcome to MT3MNA

Who are we?

MT3MNA is a fast food restaurant located in Cairo, Egypt. We started in 2015 with one small branch and one goal: to serve the best burgers in the city at a price everyone can afford.

Today we have 3 branches and hundreds of happy customers every day. We use fresh ingredients and make everything to order, so your food is always hot and delicious.

Our Story

The restaurant was founded by a family who loved cooking at home and wanted to share that with their neighborhood. What started as a tiny kiosk grew into a full restaurant because people kept coming back!

We are proud to be a local Egyptian business. We hire from the community and source our vegetables from local farms whenever possible.

What Makes Us Special

  • Fresh beef patties - never frozen
  • Homemade sauces and recipes
  • Fast service - ready in under 10 minutes
  • Affordable prices for everyone
  • Friendly staff and clean environment
